Chastity Beauty Greed

Chastity Beauty Greed

fabric and thread, 160x50 cm, 2019

‍

The project stems from the idea of combining the technique of photography and that of thread to explore the concept of identity and time.
The immutability of the stone immortalised in the photograph meets the delicacy of the thread, subtle yet lively, which enhances the curves and lingers on the corners of the figures to find their particularities, their intrinsic uniqueness.
The triptych "Chastity Beauty Cupidity" in particular, tells the story of the Graces dating back to Roman mythology linked to the cult of nature and vegetation as well as the joy of living.

‍

‍
‍

The three goddesses are the three forms of Love:
the first, Chastity, is emphasised by the eye, a love therefore visual, platonic that will never materialise; the third, Cupidity characterised by the lips, is instead a carnal and lustful love; in the centre, united in an embrace, they become Beauty, a balanced love between mind and body that leads to the expression of the most whole part of the person, hidden and pure, that which the embroidery creates beyond the stone, the heart.

‍

‍

Exhibited at the exhibition Surprize, Centro Arti Visive Pescheria, Pesaro - Italy 2019
